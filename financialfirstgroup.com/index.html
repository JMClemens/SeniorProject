About

    As the senior project for our undergraduate studies at Hood College, we had been tasked to come up with a project related to our field of expertise and a reason for its execution. We, Josh Clemens & Cory Watson, decided on doing a project about people who were doing malicious things on the internet and how we would visualize this activity.
    To accomplish this, in talks with our Supervisor, Advisor, and Reader, we decided that using honeypots who be a great approach to doing this as there embedded packages and open source nature would give us great latitude for creating the project we were envisioning. As we spend most of time connected to the internet we wanted to find out what those people who have malicious intentions out there do exactly once they’ve gotten access to a comprised system and how this can be prevented in the future. Furthermore, to accomplish this we set up three different honeypots that use alternate methods of transferring data and establishing connections to collect attacker’s data as to see what they’re exactly up to.
